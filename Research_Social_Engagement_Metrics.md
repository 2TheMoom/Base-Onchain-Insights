# Research: Social Engagement & On-Chain Activity 🔍

### Objective
To bridge the gap between social identity (Farcaster/X) and on-chain behavior on the Base network.

### Key Metrics to Track
1. **Transaction Frequency vs. Social Posting:** Do active Farcaster users transact more on-chain?
2. **NFT Minting Behavior:** Tracking "Base Paint" or "Base Origins" holders and their social reach.
3. **Liquidity Provision:** Identifying if top social influencers are also providing liquidity to Base DEXs.

### Potential Data Sources
* **Dune Analytics:** For on-chain transaction logs.
* **Neynar API:** For Farcaster social data.
* **Airstack:** For cross-chain and social identity mapping.

*Note: This research will form the logic for the upcoming Base Activity Tracker mini-app.*
