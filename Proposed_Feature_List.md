# 🚀 Social Engagement Analyzer: Proposed Features

This document outlines the core features for the final mini-app development phase.

### 1. The "Based" Reputation Score
* A dynamic 1-100 score calculated by combining:
    * **On-chain consistency:** Frequency of transactions over 30/90/180 days.
    * **Contract Variety:** Interaction with multiple protocols (DeFi, NFTs, Social).
    * **Wallet Age:** Rewards long-term network participants.

### 2. Community Badge Detection
* Auto-identification of specific community memberships:
    * **Base Paint Artist:** Detection of 'Base Paint' minting/interaction.
    * **On-chain Summer Alumni:** Identification of early network adopters.
    * **Guild Member:** Integration with Base Guild status.

### 3. Social Activity Mapper
* Mapping Ethereum addresses to Farcaster/X handles to visualize the "Social Graph" of the Base ecosystem.
* Identifying "Key Opinion Builders" (KOBs) who are active both socially and technically.

### 4. Gas & Contribution Tracker
* Calculating total ETH spent on gas as a "Network Contribution" metric.
* Highlighting users who provide liquidity to Base-native DEXs like Aerodrome.
