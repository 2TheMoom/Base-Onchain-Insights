# 🏛️ Project Manifesto: The Value of Being "Based"

The Social Engagement Analyzer was born from a simple belief: **On-chain consistency is the new resume.**

### 1. Beyond the Speculation
While much of the crypto world focuses on price, we focus on **participation**. Being "Based" isn't about how much you hold; it's about how much you contribute to the heartbeat of the network.

### 2. Rewarding the Habit
The "Infinity Flame" (240+ days) isn't just a badge. It’s a testament to the builders, the voters, and the users who show up every single day, rain or shine, to move the Superchain forward.

### 3. Open & Permissionless
Reputation should not be locked behind centralized silos. By using open SQL queries and public Dune Analytics data, we ensure that anyone can verify their own "Basedness" without asking for permission.

### 4. Security as a Right
We believe users should never have to risk their assets to prove their loyalty. Our "Read-Only" architecture is our promise to the community that your safety is our ceiling, not our floor.

### 5. Our Goal
To turn "Based" from a meme into a measurable standard of excellence.
