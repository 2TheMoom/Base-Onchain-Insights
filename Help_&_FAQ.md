# ❓ Help & FAQ: Social Engagement Analyzer

Got questions about your Based Score? We have answers.

### 1. Why is my "Based Score" lower than expected?
The score focuses on **consistency** and **diversity**. Using multiple protocols (Uniswap, Aerodrome, Base Paint) consistently over time yields a higher score than high-volume single-app usage.

### 2. My 240-day streak isn't showing. Why?
The "Infinity Flame" requires a transaction every 24 hours. A single missed day resets the on-chain streak. Verify your history on Base Scan if you believe there is a discrepancy.

### 3. Which wallets are supported?
We prioritize **Rabby Wallet** for its simulation security, but we also support Coinbase Wallet and MetaMask via **ConnectKit**.

### 4. Is this app safe?
Yes. The analyzer is **Read-Only**. We never request permission to move funds or access private keys.

### 5. When does the data refresh?
Our connection to the Dune Analytics API refreshes every 24 hours. Recent transactions may take a day to reflect in your score.
