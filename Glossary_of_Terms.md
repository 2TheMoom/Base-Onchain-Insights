# 📖 Glossary of Terms: Social Engagement Analyzer

Understanding the language of the Base ecosystem and our reputation engine.

### 1. "Based" (Reputation)
* **Definition:** A measure of a user's positive contribution, consistency, and alignment with the Base network values.
* **In-App:** Represented by your "Based Score" (0-100).

### 2. Infinity Flame (Streak)
* **Definition:** Our highest tier of engagement recognition.
* **Requirement:** Maintaining at least one on-chain transaction every 24 hours for 240+ consecutive days.

### 3. L2 (Layer 2)
* **Definition:** A secondary framework or protocol built on top of an existing blockchain (Layer 1, like Ethereum).
* **Context:** Base is an L2 designed to bring the next billion users on-chain with low fees and high speed.

### 4. Gas Analytics
* **Definition:** The study of transaction fees paid by a user to execute smart contracts.
* **Usage:** We use this to calculate a user's "Economic Weight" within the network.

### 5. Protocol Diversity
* **Definition:** The variety of different decentralized applications (dApps) a user interacts with.
* **Goal:** To encourage users to explore the full breadth of the Base ecosystem beyond simple transfers.

### 6. Read-Only Integration
* **Definition:** A security standard where an app only requests "viewing" permissions.
* **Security:** This ensures your funds remain safe while we analyze your public on-chain history.
